When you hover over the triangles, one triangle wille move out of the picture. One triangle will move in the frame. When you click on the triangle, it will change to a random color.

  • 🠕 Hover the triangles

The stars are falling in an infinite loop. Some have different keyframes, others just needed a delay. You can pause the stars by clicking on them. If you click again, you can start them falling.

  • 🠕 Click the stars to stop them
  • 🠕Click the stars to start them again.
